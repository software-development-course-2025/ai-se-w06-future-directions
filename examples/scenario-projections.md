# Scenario Projections (3 scenarios)

## Scenario A — Conservative adoption (2025-2027)
- Adoption: targeted pilots, human-in-loop
- Impact: incremental productivity improvements
- Risk: low

## Scenario B — Accelerated adoption (2025-2029)
- Adoption: broad adoption across teams, strong tooling ecosystem
- Impact: major reduction in repetitive tasks, improved dev velocity
- Risk: medium (governance & model drift)

## Scenario C — Disruptive transformation (2026-2030)
- Adoption: platform-level agent orchestration, regulatory scrutiny
- Impact: redefinition of roles, high automation
- Risk: high (ethics, regulation, dependency)
